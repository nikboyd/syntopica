Stakeholders include:

* [Governors][governor], who _govern_ a business and establish its policies
* [Requestors][requestor], who _oversee_ business activities and _request_ improvements in those activities
* [Expectors][expector], who _perform_ those activities and expect a solution to improve those activities
* [Developers][developer], who _develop_ software solutions to _improve_ business activities
